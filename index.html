Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

Rapid Form AutoFiller is a Chrome Extension designed to help developers and testers fill forms with realistic fake data. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All data used by the extension is generated locally and never leaves your browser.

  • ❌ No form data is saved
  • ❌ No real user data is accessed or used
  • ❌ No analytics, tracking, or cookies
  • ❌ No internet access or remote data transmission

2. Offline Functionality

This extension works entirely offline. All operations are performed locally within your browser without requiring an internet connection or communicating with any server.

3. Permissions

The extension only requests permissions needed to operate properly on form pages (e.g., reading and modifying form fields). It does not access your browsing history, passwords, or any other sensitive browser data.

4. Data Safety

All data used or generated is:

  • Randomly generated using fake placeholders
  • Not derived from or related to your real personal information
  • Never stored on any server or shared with any third party

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with the date of last update noted at the top.
